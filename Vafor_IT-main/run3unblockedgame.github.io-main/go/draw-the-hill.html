Get ready to embark on an exciting journey in Draw The Hill, a browser-based game that challenges players to draw paths for their cars while collecting coins and avoiding obstacles. With simple yet addictive gameplay, Draw The Hill offers endless fun as you strive to see how far you can go!

Instructions

In Draw The Hill, your objective is to click and drag to draw a path for your car. Collect coins along the way to upgrade your vehicle, but be sure to steer clear of the pipes that obstruct your path. How far can you push your car's limits?

Tips for Success

To maximize your chances of success, aim to keep your path as smooth as possible. Avoid sharp turns and abrupt changes in direction to prevent your car from getting stuck between two lines. Remember, just like in real life, potholes can spell the end of your ride. Strategize your path carefully to overcome obstacles and reach new distances!
