Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in "Awesome Tanks 2":

Controls

WASD or arrow keys to move
Left mouse button to shoot
Number keys to switch weapon

With intuitive controls at your fingertips, you'll be able to maneuver your tank with precision and unleash a barrage of firepower upon your enemies.
