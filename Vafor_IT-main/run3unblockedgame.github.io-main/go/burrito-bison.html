Embark on a wild and exhilarating adventure with Burrito Bison, an addictive browser-based game that combines elements of launching, bouncing, and smashing to create an unforgettable gaming experience. With its colorful graphics and intuitive gameplay, Burrito Bison offers endless entertainment suitable for playing on a web browser.

Instructions

In Burrito Bison, your goal is to launch yourself out of the ring by clicking anywhere to stop the spinner. Once airborne, smash gummies to earn money, which you can use to purchase upgrades to enhance your abilities. Click during flight or press the space bar to activate your rocket boost and soar even higher into the air. Master the art of launching and bouncing to achieve the highest scores and conquer new challenges.

Smash and Soar

Join Burrito Bison on his quest to escape the clutches of the gummy empire and reclaim his stolen wallet. As you progress through the game, face off against increasingly challenging obstacles and enemies. Strategize your moves and upgrades to overcome obstacles and launch yourself to new heights. With each successful jump, earn rewards and unlock new features to aid you on your journey.
