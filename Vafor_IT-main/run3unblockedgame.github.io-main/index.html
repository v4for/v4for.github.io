Run 3 Unblocked: Endless Fun in Space!

Run 3 Unblocked, the brainchild of Joseph Cloutier, propels you into a thrilling cosmic odyssey. As the third iteration in the Run series, it's now available in HTML5, ensuring seamless play without the need for Flash support. Your mission? Guide your intrepid alien through ever-shifting terrain, evading dangers and pushing your limits.

In this heart-pounding endless runner game, your goal is straightforward: run relentlessly and avoid pitfalls. Navigate through a maze of obstacles including treacherous gaps, collapsing tiles, and cunning traps. The twist? The environment itself morphs, challenging your reflexes and strategic thinking.

Embrace the challenge with these controls:

  • Arrows: Move
  • Space or Up Arrow: Jump
  • R: Reset
  • P: Pause

No matter your device, Run 3 Unblocked delivers gripping gameplay. Whether on your PC, mobile phone, or tablet, the absence of Flash won't hinder your cosmic adventure. Dive in and immerse yourself in the thrill of the chase!

The Origins of Run 3 Unblocked

Joseph Cloutier, the visionary mind behind Run 3 Unblocked, hails from the USA. Building on the success of the earlier installments, Cloutier crafted an experience that captivates players worldwide.
